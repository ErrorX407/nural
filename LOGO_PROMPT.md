# Nural Logo Prompt

**Concept:**
A minimal, abstract representation of a "neural node" or "connection point" that evokes intelligence, speed, and structure.

**Visual Elements:**

- **Shape:** A central hexagon or circle with 3-4 distinct branching lines (synapses) connecting to smaller outer nodes, forming a network or a letter 'N'.
- **Style:** Geometric, flat, modern, tech-focused. No gradients (or very subtle ones).
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: White (#FFFFFF)
  - Background: Solid Black (#000000)
- **Vibe:** Ultra-Minimalist, Iconic, "The Core".

**Midjourney / DALL-E Prompt (The "Next.js" Style N):**

> **Subject:** Minimalist N Logo.
> **Visual:** A bold white sans-serif letter 'N' on a pitch black background. The 'N' features a subtle, high-end "fade" or gradient mask on the diagonal stroke, similar to the Vercel/Next.js brand.
> **Shape:** Sharp, geometric, futuristic. Enclosed in a circle or shield (optional).
> **Style:** High-contrast, tech-first, clean typography as a logo mark.
> **Technical:** --no text --v 5.2 --style raw

**Alternative Prompt (Express Interpretation):**

> A simple white bracket or parens symbol forming an abstract 'N'. Coding aesthetic. Minimalist monospaced font style. Solid black background.
